Listening Test: Articulatory Speech Synthesis

About This Study

We are evaluating audio generated by a physics-based speech synthesizer that models the human vocal tract. You will listen to short audio clips (single words or short phrases) and rate their quality.

Duration: approximately 12-15 minutes.

Requirements:

  • You must use headphones (not speakers)
  • You must be in a quiet environment
  • You must be a fluent English speaker

What you will do:

  1. Complete a brief headphone check (3 trials)
  2. Part 1: Rate the overall quality of 24 audio samples on a 1-5 scale
  3. Part 2: For 8 pairs, choose which sample sounds closer to a reference

Your responses are anonymous. Data will be used for academic research only.
